There's something special about getting out early before the rest of the world wakes up.

## The Trail

Started at dawn, about 6 AM. The air was crisp and the trail was empty. Just me and the sounds of the forest waking up.

## Highlights

- Spotted some deer near the creek
- Great views from the ridge
- Made it back before lunch

## Stats

- **Distance**: 8 miles
- **Elevation gain**: 1,200 ft
- **Time**: 4 hours
